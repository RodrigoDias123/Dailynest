About DailyNest

DailyNest is built around a single idea: your work shouldn't be scattered across a dozen different tools. We bring tasks, calendar events, notes, and file storage together in one clean, distraction-free workspace.

Our task manager lets you create, prioritise, and track every action item — whether it's a quick errand or a multi-week project. Status badges keep your team aligned without lengthy status meetings.

The integrated agenda gives you a weekly overview of every commitment, with colour-coded events that make scheduling conflicts impossible to miss.

The notepad is your lightweight writing space — perfect for meeting notes, brainstorming sessions, or capturing fleeting ideas before they disappear.

Files keeps your documents exactly where you need them: associated with your workspace and always a single click away. No more hunting through email attachments or cloud folder hierarchies.
