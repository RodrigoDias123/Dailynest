Agenda

Never miss what matters.

A full calendar that keeps your schedule clear. See events in Day, Week, or Month view and know exactly what's coming next.

  • Day, Week, and Month views with smooth switching
  • Color-coded events for instant visual clarity
  • Mini calendar for fast date navigation
  • Upcoming events panel so nothing slips through
Notepad

Every idea, instantly saved.

A distraction-free editor that auto-saves as you type. Your notes live alongside everything else — no app switching needed.

  • Auto-saves on every keystroke — no manual save required
  • Two-panel layout: note list on the left, editor on the right
  • Word count shown live as you write
  • Unlimited notes, always organized by date

Files

Your cloud, always with you.

Upload any file and access it alongside your tasks and notes. No third-party storage, no integrations — it's all built in.

Workspaces

Work and life, in balance.

One toggle separates your professional and personal world. Tasks, notes, events, and files stay neatly in their lane — automatically.

  • Switch workspaces with a single click in the sidebar
  • All four modules — tasks, agenda, notes, files — respect the active workspace
  • Your last workspace is remembered between sessions
  • No confusion, no mixing, no clutter
